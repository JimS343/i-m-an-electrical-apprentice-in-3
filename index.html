Built for Canadian electrical apprentices

A study website organized around the Red Seal major work activities.

This version of the site is centered on the categories that shape trade training and Red Seal review. That keeps your notes organized by how the trade is actually tested and practiced instead of turning into a pile of random topics.

Open the MWA library How to use this site

Why this structure works

Organizing by Red Seal major work activity gives you a cleaner long-term system. It helps you file school notes, jobsite reminders, and exam prep under the same headings.

How to build it

Add your notes in small chunks. If you learn something about grounding, conduit fill, or motor controls, put it under the MWA where it belongs and keep moving.

Important reminder

Treat this as a study aid, not a replacement for the Canadian Electrical Code, provincial rules, manufacturer instructions, or your instructor and journeyperson guidance.
